SilkSong is a highly anticipated sequel to the critically acclaimed game Hollow Knight, developed by Team Cherry.

Set in a vast and beautifully handcrafted world, players take on the role of Hornet, a skilled warrior princess, as she embarks on an epic journey filled with challenging platforming, intense combat, and intricate exploration.

With its stunning visuals, hauntingly atmospheric soundtrack, and deep lore, SilkSong promises to captivate players with its immersive gameplay experience and continue the legacy of Hollow Knight as a beloved indie masterpiece.
