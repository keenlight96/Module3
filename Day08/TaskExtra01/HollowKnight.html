Hollow Knight

Release date: 25 Feb, 2017

Hollow Knight is an enchanting action-adventure game developed by Team Cherry. Set in the forsaken kingdom of Hallownest, players take on the role of a nameless Knight, exploring a vast underground world teeming with danger and intrigue. The game combines stunning hand-drawn visuals, atmospheric music, and precise gameplay mechanics to create an unforgettable experience.

As the Knight, players navigate through interconnected areas, battling a variety of challenging foes and unraveling the secrets of the kingdom. Along the way, they encounter memorable characters, uncover hidden lore, and acquire new abilities that enhance their combat and traversal skills. With its immersive world, engaging gameplay, and a sense of melancholic beauty, Hollow Knight has earned widespread acclaim, becoming a beloved gem in the indie gaming landscape.

SilkSong

Release date: TBD

Silksong is an upcoming action-adventure game being developed by Team Cherry, the creators of Hollow Knight. Serving as a sequel to the original game, Silksong takes players on a brand new journey with a different protagonist, Hornet. Hornet, a fierce and agile warrior, finds herself trapped in a twisted kingdom known as Pharloom. Players must guide her through this captivating and treacherous world, brimming with new challenges, intriguing characters, and stunning environments.

In Silksong, players will navigate through intricately designed levels, engaging in fast-paced combat with a diverse range of enemies. Hornet possesses her own unique set of skills and abilities, allowing for dynamic and fluid combat encounters. The game promises to offer an expanded move set, new tools, and a vast array of enemies to overcome. With its atmospheric art style, haunting music, and the promise of a compelling narrative, Silksong is highly anticipated by fans of the original game and is expected to deliver another captivating adventure in the rich universe of Hollow Knight.

Team Cherry

About us

Team Cherry is an independent video game development studio based in Adelaide, South Australia. The studio was founded in 2014 by Ari Gibson, William Pellen, and David Kazi, who shared a passion for creating unique and immersive gaming experiences. Team Cherry gained significant recognition and acclaim for their debut game, Hollow Knight, which was released in 2017.

In addition to its successful release, Hollow Knight also received several awards and nominations, including the BAFTA Games Award for Best Debut Game in 2019. The success of Hollow Knight allowed Team Cherry to expand and further develop their studio.

Team Cherry has a reputation for their attention to detail, hand-drawn art style, and commitment to creating rich and immersive worlds. They are known for their dedication to creating games with tight and precise gameplay mechanics, coupled with captivating narratives and memorable characters.
